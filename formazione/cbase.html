🛡️ Cyber Awareness Course

Essential training for anyone who works with a computer every day. Even if you don’t work in cybersecurity, you engage with it every time you read an email or share a file.

🎯 Course Objective

Help employees identify digital threats and adopt safe behaviors in daily use of emails, devices, and digital tools.

📚 Course Program (20 hours)

    - The cyber domain and the user’s role -
    - Digital deception: the human factor -
    - Phishing, smishing, and online scams -
    - Passwords, credentials, and digital identity -
    - Company devices: proper and safe usage -
    - Web browsing and digital tool usage -
    - Data, documents, and personal responsibility -
    - Updates, antivirus, and digital protection -
    - Simulation: a "risky" workday -
    - Wrap-up and operational summary -

📦 Included in the Course

    - PowerPoint slides and learning materials -
    - Desk-friendly summary infographics -
    - Digital handbook with security checklists -
